## 5. FAQ Answers (long-form, for SEO page or expanded FAQ)

### Do you track flights for Dublin Airport transfers?
Yes. We monitor every flight in real time using flight tracking software integrated with
our dispatch system. If your plane lands 20 minutes early or two hours late, we adjust
your pickup automatically. You don't need to call or send a message — we handle it.

### What if my flight is delayed?
We wait up to 60 minutes from your actual landing time at no extra charge. If you are
delayed beyond 60 minutes, additional waiting time is charged at €15 per 30-minute block.
We'll always message you before any extra charge applies, so there are no surprises on
your invoice.

### How do I pay?
We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), cash,
and bank transfer. Approved corporate accounts receive itemised monthly invoices with a
full VAT breakdown — every journey is ready for your expense report the moment it ends.

### Is VAT included in the price?
Yes. Every price shown on our website and in your quote includes Irish VAT at the
applicable rate. Your receipt or invoice will show the full VAT breakdown so you can
reclaim it on business journeys without any extra administration.

### Can I book a transfer for a client or guest?
Yes, this is common for PA bookings and hotel concierge requests. Enter your own
details as the booker, then add your guest's name and mobile number in the notes field
when completing the booking form. We'll send the driver's details and a map pin directly
to your guest before the journey.

### What is the cancellation policy?
Cancellations made more than 24 hours before your scheduled pickup are free of charge.
Cancellations within 24 hours of the scheduled pickup are charged at 50% of the agreed
fare. No-shows — where the driver has arrived at the correct location and the client is
not reachable after 15 minutes — are charged at the full fare. We'll always attempt to
contact you by phone and WhatsApp before marking a job as a no-show.

### Do you offer corporate accounts?
Yes. Corporate accounts receive monthly invoicing with itemised VAT breakdowns, priority
booking access during busy periods, and volume discounts for regular users. To apply,
email info@vitalychauffeur.com with your company name and estimated monthly journey
volume — we typically respond within one business day.

### What areas outside Dublin do you cover?
We cover all of Ireland. Frequently requested long-distance routes and their approximate
flat rates:
- Dublin → Cork: approx. €350 incl. VAT
- Dublin → Galway: approx. €280 incl. VAT
- Dublin → Belfast: approx. €250 incl. VAT
- Dublin → Limerick: approx. €220 incl. VAT

Contact us for an exact quote on any route. Long-distance bookings require 24 hours'
notice where possible.
